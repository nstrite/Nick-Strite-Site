About Nick Strite

A northwest based freelance photojournalist, Nick was born in Kansas City, Kansas and has lived through out the United States. He graduated from The Evergreen State College in 2012 and continues to live in Olympia, Wash. Nick has done freelance work for several magazines including AMP and AP. He was an intern in 2009 learning from award winning photojournalist Tony Overman at The Olympian. Nick Strite intends to continue with his photojournalism career after college and plans to attend graduate school.

Nick's Core Principles

Nick's adhears to three strict principles as a journalist:

  • Strong ethics: Adhearing to core journalist ethics remaining outside the bias.
  • Be there or be square: Striving to be punctual and active in the community, paying close attention to the community.
  • Style and Technique: Keeping his skills sharp and his eye steadfast on the world around him to create the best image possible.
